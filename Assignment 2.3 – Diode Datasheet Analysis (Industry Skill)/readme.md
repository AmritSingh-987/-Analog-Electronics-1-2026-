## Why the 1N4148?

The 1N4148 is one of the most widely used small-signal diodes in the world. Its combination of fast switching speed (4 ns recovery time), low forward voltage, and robust temperature range make it the default choice when a designer needs a diode that "just works" in logic-level and signal-conditioning circuits. It is not intended for power rectification — that role belongs to diodes like the 1N4007 — but for anything below ~100 mA at high frequency, it is nearly ideal.
